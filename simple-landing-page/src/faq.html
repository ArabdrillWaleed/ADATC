Training FAQ

When was the Arabian Drilling Training Center established?

A: The Arabian Drilling Training Center (ADATC) was established in 1993 at the Dhahran base. It was the first center in Saudi Arabia to deliver both Technical and HSE training.

What types of courses are offered?

A: To learn more about our wide range of training courses and accreditations, simply select the Training Offerings link located at the top of this page or click HERE for a quick Overview.

What types of instructors are available?

- Technical Instructors: For drilling and technical topics.
- Well Control Instructors: Specialized in well control training.
- HSE Instructors: Focused on Health, Safety, and Environment.
- Soft Skills Instructor: For communication and leadership skills.
- Driving Training Instructors: For safe driving practices.

A: You can search by name or role using the search function on the Meet Our Team page.
