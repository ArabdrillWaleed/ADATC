Driving Value

Our Vision

Arabian Drilling Academy Training Center (ADATC) aims to be the leading training institution and preferred partner for industry leaders in Saudi Arabia and the region, recognized for its outstanding quality of education and hands-on training in the energy sector.​

Delivering Excellence

Our Mission

Our mission is to cultivate excellence in technical knowledge, operational proficiency, and safety practices through innovative curricula, advanced facilities and strategic partnerships with leading companies and academic institutions. We aim to develop a highly skilled and competent workforce for the oil and gas industry in Saudi Arabia and the region.​
